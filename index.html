Why uSkyBlock

Built for Skyblock servers that need real gameplay depth, long-term maintainability, and room to customize.

Gameplay that scales

Build around islands, progression, challenges, and party play instead of a minimal starter setup.

Deep customization

Tune gameplay with challenges, biome options, schematics, and server-focused configuration.

Modern maintenance

Run a project with more than a decade of lineage that still targets current Paper and Spigot servers.

Global-ready

Support an international player base with 53 bundled locale files and an active translation workflow.

Open and extensible

Use public docs, a public API, and a documented developer workflow to extend or integrate responsibly.

Open-source lineage

Built on a long community-maintained history with public docs, public code, and a public API.
